New clients

If you are a new client, you can view available openings on my client portal, but you will need to request one of these openings by phone, (531) 222-6371.

Before you call

When you call to set your appointment, I will need your insurance information:

  • Member ID number
  • Phone number for providers to call
  • Plan name, if on your card
  • Payer ID (a 5-digit number), if on your card

You may want me to coordinate care with another provider, such as your psychiatrist, primary care physician, etc. If so, please complete this form to authorize release of behavioral health information.

Before your first appointment

First appointments last from 1-1/2 to 2 hours; please fill out these forms prior to your session (allow 45 minutes to complete):

  • Informed Consent, Professional Disclosure, and Policies
  • Consent to Treatment
  • New Client Information Form

As a client, you are entitled to a copy of Healing & Wellness Counseling's Privacy Practices.
